About

The research at Realistic 3D is on Multidimensional Imaging and Visualization with focus on Multiscopic 3D Capture, Coding and Visualization. The research group Realistic 3D is developing future 3D visualization for entertainment and industry, focusing on improving capture, compression, and presentation technology by making use of sophisticated signal and image processing.
